About our business


STARLIGHT is a tunisian local brand created by Ahmed Gadhrib that specializes in creating and selling lamps


Our products are all handmade and crafted from bottles collected from the beach in order to repurpose them while also saving the enviroment

Our objectives and goals


Our biggest aim is to help clean our planet and while simultaneously brightening up your day with our beautiful lamps that shines through your day
